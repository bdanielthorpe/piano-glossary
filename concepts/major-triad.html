Major Triad

Foundation concept

The major triad is the most fundamental chord in Western harmony — built by stacking a minor third on top of a major third.

Formula:

1 · 3 · 5

Intervals:

M3 · m3

Example (Cmaj):

C · E · G

Example voicing:

C · G · E

Minor 7th Chord

maj7

Prerequisites Minor triad

A minor 7th chord is a minor triad with an added minor seventh interval. This is most easily thought of as living a tone below the root.

Formula

1 · b3 · 5 · b7

Intervals:

m3 · M3 · m3

Example (Cmin):

C · Eb · G · Bb

Example voicing:

C · G · Bb · Eb

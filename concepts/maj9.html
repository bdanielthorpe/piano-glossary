Major 9th Chord

maj9

Prerequisites Major 7th chord

The major ninth chord is constructed by adding the ninth to a major seventh chord.

Formula:

1 · 3 · 5 · 7 · 9

The major ninth chord can be thought of as a minor 7th chord stacked on the root:

R · min7

Example (Cmaj9):

C · E · G · B · D

Example voicing:

C · B · D · E · G

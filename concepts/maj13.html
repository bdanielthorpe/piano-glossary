Major 13th Chord

maj13

Prerequisites Major 7th chord

The major 13th chord is constructed by adding the 13th to a major seventh chord.

Formula:

1 · 3 · 5 · 7 · 13

Example (Cmaj13):

C · E · G · B · A

Example voicing:

C · E · A · B · D

1 · 3 · 13 · 7 · 9

Since chords are named by their highest extension, this chord may also include the 9th.

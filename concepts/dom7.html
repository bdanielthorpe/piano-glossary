Dominant 7th Chord

dom7

Prerequisites Major triad

The dominant seventh chord is a major triad with an added minor seventh. It occurs on the fifth degree of the major scale.

Formula:

1 · 3 · 5 · b7

Intervals:

M3 · m3 · m3

Example (E7):

E · G# · B · D

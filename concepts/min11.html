Minor 11th Chord

m11

Prerequisites Minor 7th chord

The minor 11th chord is constructed by adding the 11th to a minor seventh chord.

Formula:

1 · b3 · 5 · b7 · 11

Example (Cmaj9):

C · E · G · B · F

Example voicing:

C · E · F · Bb · D

1 · 3 · 11 · b7 · 9

Since chords are named by their highest extension, this chord may also include the 9th.

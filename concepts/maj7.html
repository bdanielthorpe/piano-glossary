Major 7th Chord

maj7

Prerequisites Major triad

Take a major triad and add the note a semitone below the octave. That's the major 7th — and it transforms a stable triad into something much more ambiguous and unstable.

Formula:

1 · 3 · 5 · 7

Intervals:

M3 · m3 · M3

Example (Cmaj7):

C · E · G · B

Example voicing:

C · G · B · E
